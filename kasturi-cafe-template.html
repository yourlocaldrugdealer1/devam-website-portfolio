Our story

Started in a 200 sq. ft. corner, grown one regular at a time.

Kasturi opened in 2019 with four tables and a single espresso machine. No investors, no franchise playbook — just a belief that Ahmedabad deserved a café where the coffee was taken as seriously as the conversation around it.

Six years on, the tables have grown to eighteen, but the rule hasn't changed: every cup is pulled to order, every loaf is baked before sunrise, and every regular's usual is remembered without asking.
