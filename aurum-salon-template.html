Our story

Eight years, one standard.

Aurum opened in 2018 in Satellite with a single belief: that a premium salon experience shouldn't require a five-star hotel bill. Expert hands, quality products, and a calm space — that's all it takes.

We've grown from a four-chair studio to a full-service space for 12,000+ clients across Ahmedabad — but the approach hasn't changed. No upselling, no rushing, no compromises on the products we use on your hair and skin.

Every product at Aurum is sourced from certified professional brands. We use ammonia-free colour lines, Rica wax, and Dermalogica skincare — the same products used in premium salons across Mumbai and Delhi.
